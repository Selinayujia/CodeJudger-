Design and implement a program to solve SUDOKU puzzles. More detail in NYU Classes assignment.

The game board consists of 9 x 9 cells divided into smaller blocks of 3 x 3 cells.

The goal is to find assignments (1 though 9) for the empty cells such that every row, every column,
and every 3 x 3 block contains each of the digits from 1 to 9.

Input: 
Initial cell values (0 means blank cell)

Output:
Your Solution
